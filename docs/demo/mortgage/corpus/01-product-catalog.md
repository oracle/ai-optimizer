# Mortgage Product Catalog

This catalog describes the mortgage products currently offered. Each product is identified by an internal `product_code` used across all bank systems.

---

## FIXED30-PRIME — 30-Year Fixed Prime

A standard 30-year fixed-rate mortgage for prime borrowers.

- **Product code:** `FIXED30-PRIME`
- **Rate type:** Fixed for the full 30-year term
- **Current base rate:** 6.500% APR
- **Minimum credit score:** 720
- **Maximum loan-to-value (LTV):** 80%
- **Typical loan size:** $200,000 to $1,500,000
- **Origination fee:** 0.75% of loan amount
- **Prepayment penalty:** None after the first 36 months

### Best for

Prime-credit borrowers who want predictable monthly payments and intend to stay in the home for at least seven years. Because the rate is fixed for the full term, this product protects against future rate increases but is more expensive than ARM products at origination.

### Underwriting notes

Borrowers below the 720 credit-score floor must be processed under the exception process described in the Underwriting Policy. See section "Exception Approvals" for details.

## ARM5_1-STD — 5/1 Adjustable-Rate Standard

An adjustable-rate mortgage with a five-year fixed introductory period.

- **Product code:** `ARM5_1-STD`
- **Rate type:** Fixed for 5 years, then adjusts annually
- **Current base rate:** 5.875% APR
- **Index after fixed period:** SOFR + 2.75% margin, capped at 2% annual / 5% lifetime adjustment
- **Minimum credit score:** 680
- **Maximum LTV:** 85%
- **Typical loan size:** $150,000 to $900,000
- **Origination fee:** 0.50% of loan amount
- **Prepayment penalty:** 1% of remaining principal in first 24 months, then none

### Best for

Borrowers who plan to sell, refinance, or substantially pay down the loan within five to seven years. The lower introductory rate translates to meaningful monthly savings during the fixed period, but borrowers carry interest-rate risk after year five.

## JUMBO15-PRIME — 15-Year Jumbo Prime

A 15-year fixed-rate jumbo mortgage for high-value properties.

- **Product code:** `JUMBO15-PRIME`
- **Rate type:** Fixed for the full 15-year term
- **Current base rate:** 6.250% APR
- **Minimum credit score:** 760
- **Maximum LTV:** 75%
- **Minimum loan size:** $766,550 (above the conforming loan limit)
- **Maximum loan size:** $3,000,000
- **Origination fee:** 1.00% of loan amount
- **Reserves required:** 12 months of PITI in liquid assets
- **Prepayment penalty:** None

### Best for

High-income borrowers purchasing or refinancing a primary residence in a high-cost market who want to be debt-free in 15 years and can comfortably absorb the higher monthly payment.

## FHA-30YR — 30-Year FHA

An FHA-insured 30-year fixed-rate mortgage for borrowers who do not qualify for our prime products.

- **Product code:** `FHA-30YR`
- **Rate type:** Fixed for 30 years
- **Current base rate:** 6.750% APR
- **Minimum credit score:** 580 (with 3.5% down) or 500 (with 10% down)
- **Maximum LTV:** 96.5%
- **Mortgage insurance premium (MIP):** Required for the life of the loan
- **Origination fee:** 1.00% of loan amount

### Best for

First-time homebuyers and borrowers with limited down payment savings or credit scores below the FIXED30-PRIME threshold. FHA insurance allows the bank to underwrite loans we could not otherwise approve, but the lifetime MIP makes this the most expensive product over a long horizon.
