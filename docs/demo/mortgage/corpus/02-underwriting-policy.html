Mortgage Underwriting Policy

This document defines the underwriting standards applied to all mortgage applications. Loan officers must verify each applicant against these standards before submitting for approval.

1. Credit-score floors

The following minimum FICO credit scores are required, by product:

Product code Minimum FICO
FIXED30-PRIME 720
ARM5_1-STD 680
JUMBO15-PRIME 760
FHA-30YR 580

The applicable score is the middle of the three scores reported by Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion. For joint applications, the lower of the two primary applicants' middle scores governs.

Applications below the floor for the requested product must either be steered to a more appropriate product or routed through the exception process (Section 6).

2. Loan-to-value (LTV) limits

Maximum LTV is calculated as the loan principal divided by the lesser of the appraised value or the contract sale price.

Product code Max LTV
FIXED30-PRIME 80%
ARM5_1-STD 85%
JUMBO15-PRIME 75%
FHA-30YR 96.5%

LTV above 80% on conventional products requires private mortgage insurance (PMI). FHA loans require the FHA mortgage insurance premium (MIP) regardless of LTV.

3. Debt-to-income (DTI) ratio

DTI is calculated as total monthly debt obligations (including the new mortgage's principal, interest, taxes, and insurance) divided by gross monthly income.

Product code Max DTI
FIXED30-PRIME 43%
ARM5_1-STD 45%
JUMBO15-PRIME 40%
FHA-30YR 50%

Applicants with DTI within 2 percentage points of the maximum may be approved with documented compensating factors (twelve months of reserves, stable employment of five-plus years, or significant non-retirement liquid assets).

4. Income and employment verification

  • W-2 employees: most recent two years of W-2s plus 30 days of pay stubs.
  • Self-employed: most recent two years of personal and business tax returns; year-to-date P&L for the current year.
  • Bonus and commission income may be counted only if it has been received for at least 24 months and is expected to continue.
  • Rental income from existing investment properties is counted at 75% of gross rents.

5. Reserve requirements

Liquid reserves (excluding retirement accounts) required after closing:

Product code Required reserves
FIXED30-PRIME 2 months of PITI
ARM5_1-STD 2 months of PITI
JUMBO15-PRIME 12 months of PITI
FHA-30YR 0 months (none required)

PITI = principal, interest, taxes, insurance.

6. Exception approvals

Applications that miss one underwriting threshold may be considered for exception approval if all of the following hold:

  1. The miss is on a single criterion only — not multiple simultaneously.
  2. The applicant exceeds at least two other thresholds by a meaningful margin (for example, DTI 10+ points below the cap, or reserves of 24+ months).
  3. A senior underwriter (Level 3 or above) signs off in writing.

Exception approvals are reviewed monthly by the credit-risk committee. Loan officers should not promise an exception during pre-qualification.

7. Re-qualification of existing borrowers

Existing borrowers seeking a new mortgage, a refinance, or a HELOC must be re-qualified using their current credit score, income, and DTI — not the values at the time of their original loan. A borrower who qualified for FIXED30-PRIME at origination but whose credit has since dropped below 720 cannot be approved for a new FIXED30-PRIME loan; they must be steered to ARM5_1-STD or FHA-30YR depending on current score and LTV.

This applies even when the borrower is otherwise current on their existing loan.
